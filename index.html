– a2z family lab –
Family Research and Evidence-Based Family Coaching

Welcome! The a2z family lab was born from the curiosity and drive of two dedicated researchers, Ziwen Teuber and Anke Weber, psychologists with PhDs from Germany. Founded within the Department of Behavioral and Cognitive Sciences at the University of Luxembourg, our lab explores how children, adolescents, and families navigate life and learning.
